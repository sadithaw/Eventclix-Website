Ella

Ella is a small town located in the Badulla District of Uva Province, Sri Lanka. It is situated in the hills of the southern region of the central highlands, about 1000 meters above sea level.
The town is known for its picturesque location, with stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. It is a popular tourist destination, with a number of attractions including hiking trails, waterfalls, and cultural sites.
